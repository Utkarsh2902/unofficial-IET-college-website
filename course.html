Course We Offer

Institute of Engineering and Technology (IET) was established in the year 1984. It is also known as a top engineering college in India. The institute was earlier affiliated (1984-1999) to the University of Lucknow. Currently, the college is recognised by Dr APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University and approved by All India Council for Technical Education. The admission process is done on the basis of rank obtained by candidates in UPSEE (Uttar Pradesh State Engineering Admission Test) /GATE Entrance Exam.

Undergraduate Programs

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in various branches of engineering, such as Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Civil Engineering, etc.

Postgraduate Programs

Master of Computer Applications (MCA),Master of Business Administration (MBA) Master of Technology (M.Tech) in different specializations, including Computer Science and Engineering, Digital Electronics, Power Electronics, Environmental Engineering, Structural Engineering, etc.

Management Programs

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in various branches of engineering, such as Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Civil Engineering, etc.

Self Finance Programs

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in various branches of engineering and Master of Technology(M.Tech), such as Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Microelectronics and Communication,Structural Engineering Engineering, Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, etc.

IET is constructing a modern sports complex for easy access to sports, benefiting the fitness of young engineers. It will have football, cricket, tennis, basketball, volleyball, running track, and a multipurpose hall.

Computer Center

The institute's computer center has modern hardware and software, supporting in-house software development and maintenance. Multiple labs with updated desktops facilitate experiments and act as general-purpose computer labs.

Auditorium

Pandit Ram Prasad Bismil Sabhagar seats 800 people with acoustic AV amenities, video conferencing, digital projection, and Wi-Fi. Ideal for seminars, conferences, presentations, cultural events, and ENCORE students' fest, it boasts an excellent stage, back stage support, and service room.
