The Cell & Facilities

The Placement Office is responsible for campus placement at IET Lucknow. The Placement-in- Charge, the Assistant Placement Officer, the Placement Office staff and the student representatives handle various crucial tasks in the office like reaching out to companies, scheduling & executing placement related activities and carrying out all official communication with recruiters and with students. The team strives to strike a match between recruiter expectations and student aspirations. The team of student representatives consists of the Placement Managers who coordinate the activities of other student members and ensure compliance with various institute policies; Company Coordinators who execute coordination with the recruiting organizations; and Department Placement Coordinators who steer student preparedness and employability enhancement initiatives. The Placement Office is well-equipped with excellent infrastructure to support every stage of the placement process.


The placement cell at our institution boasts a state-of-the-art workspace that caters to all official proceedings. It includes various facilities designed to ensure a seamless and productive placement process for both students and visiting companies. To begin with, we have 6 air-conditioned interview cabins specifically designated for visiting companies to conduct personal interviews or panel rounds. These cabins are equipped with high-speed internet on WiFi-enabled PCs, ensuring smooth communication and connectivity. Additionally, printing facilities are available for any necessary documentation.


For group discussions, there is a dedicated hall that accommodates placement procedure rounds by visiting companies. This space allows for constructive discussions and evaluations during the placement process. To cater to the technical requirements of recruiters, our computer center offers labs from all departments with modern and adequate facilities. These labs provide a hassle-free environment to conduct technical rounds efficiently.


LFor presentations and lectures, we have a well-connected and air-conditioned presentation room. This lecture theatre is equipped with a projection system, sound system, and live conferencing facility. It can comfortably accommodate around 120 candidates, providing an ideal setting for various presentations and interactive sessions. Furthermore, we take pride in our newly constructed state-of-the-art auditorium, which has a spacious seating capacity of 850. This auditorium serves as the venue for Pre-Placement Talks, Seminars, and Demonstrations, providing a grand space for important events.


Recognizing the significance of training, we have a separate training classroom dedicated to students from all departments. Equipped with necessary facilities and equipment, this training center aims to enhance students' technical skills and prepare them professionally for their future endeavors. In addition to the core facilities, we also extend hospitality to visiting HRs. We provide lodging and transport facilities for their convenience. Moreover, we offer a diverse choice of cuisine for meals, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable stay during their visit. With these comprehensive facilities and services, our placement cell strives to create an optimal environment for successful placements and meaningful interactions between students and potential employers.



    THE FACILITIES INCLUDE

  • Center with a capacity of 150+ Computers.
  • Auditoriums to conduct workshops and pre-placement talks
  • Tele-interview rooms
  • Video-conferencing facility
  • Air-conditioned office and interview cabins
  • Infrastructure to support online and written screening tests
